Privacy Policy for Greenbits

Last updated: May 11, 2026

1. Introduction

Greenbits ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

  • Plant Data: Information about your plants (names, photos, watering schedules) stored locally on your device.
  • Purchase Information: If you make in-app purchases, payment processing is handled by Apple. We do not store your payment details.
  • Device Information: Basic device information for app functionality and crash reporting.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide plant care reminders and tracking features
  • To process in-app purchases
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs

4. Data Storage

Your plant data is stored locally on your device. We do not upload your personal data to external servers unless you explicitly choose to back up your data.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat: For managing subscriptions and in-app purchases
  • Apple Services: For notifications and app distribution

6. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

You can delete all your data by uninstalling the app from your device.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date.
